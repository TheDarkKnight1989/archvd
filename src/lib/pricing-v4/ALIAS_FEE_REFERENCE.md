## Commission Fees

Base: **9.5%** (or 12.4% for Canadian sellers) + seller fee

### Rating-Based Tiers

| Seller Rating | Commission |
|---------------|------------|
| 90+ | 9.5% |
| 70-89 | 15% |
| 50-69 | 20% |
| Below 50 | 25% |

- Each cancellation: -10 points
- Each successful sale: +2 points

## Cash Out Fees

- **2.9%** cash out fee on selling price
- Cross-Border Fee (USD payout outside US): **0.6%**
- Minimum withdrawal: $10.00 USD

## Notes for Implementation

- All shipping fees are in USD regardless of seller region
- Commission is percentage of listing price, deducted at sale
- Cash out fee is percentage of selling price at withdrawal
- Canadian sellers have higher base commission (12.4%)
